About Us

Sinopec Technologies Limited (HK) + Dongguan Red Color Materials Co., Ltd. (丹彩日用品) — an ODM partnership serving global clients.

Who We Are

Sinopec Technologies, Ltd. was established in 2012 and entered the international trade field. The company has been specializing in producing export orders for more than ten years and ventured into the designer toy and action figure sector in 2022. With a strong team of engineers dedicated to product development and skilled workers with over ten years of production experience overseeing quality control, our products now boast a 99.5% excellent product rate. Prototypes can be customized from the design phase to mold-making and production, allowing the selection of the best materials during the prototype stage to achieve the highest product quality. The factory has dedicated clean rooms for mass production to ensure flawless final products.

Our Footprint

As an ODM in the industry, we possess strong price competitiveness and have stable raw material suppliers and partner factories. As an international trading company, we have been pursuing a global expansion strategy since our establishment. We have long-term partnerships with renowned companies, including Walmart, Target, Disney, and Melissa & Doug in the United States; Nikkan, Chica, and Mato in Japan; Bathtime in Europe; and Asura in South Africa.

Manufacturing Entity (Production)

The Dongguan factory runs complete in-house production lines — including clean rooms dedicated to mass production — covering the full journey from 3D modeling and prototyping to bulk shipment. Every product passes multi-stage QC, with a long-term pass rate above 99.5%.
